This week the students are required to make make an in-circuit programmer

This week the students are required to make make an in-circuit programmer. They exactly have to made an HELLO ISP programmer for using it during the nexts weeks for programming other boards like attiny44/45/85 based and newly including xmega type. The first have to download the hello isp traces and outcut files from the academy webpage and convert them in to srm workable files with fabmodules webbrowser MIT programm

Learn to Turn Codes into Things

After making the toolpath, the have to cut it with SRM20 Mini CNC using 1/64 and 1/32 inch size micromills.After several trials the got each one a nice good looking board that they can solder. Learning not only how to cut the pcb,also how to solder correctly very small components THIS ACADEMY WORK ON SMD TYPE OF COMPONENTS NOT TRUE HOLE!
