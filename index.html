Electronic Production

This week the students are required to make make an in-circuit programmer.

All the studen should make their own programmer so they can programm theirs future boards.They will use a precision milling cnc for cutting theirs PCBS, solder the components and get theirs first steps in to the programming world.

Computer Controlled Cutting a.k.a LaserCut

This week the students are required to design and make a corrugated cardboard press-fit construction kit .

Most of our students are trying Grasshopper as a parametric modeling tool, others try to use 123d Make. Theirs first prototype is made in normal 3mm corrugated cardboard, after they fully functional prototype they try depending on theirs personal preferences with plywood, acryl and some of them try to use also the metal laser cutter. What results/designs we will see when they finish theirs cuts?
